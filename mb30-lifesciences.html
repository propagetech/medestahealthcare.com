ABOUT US

Medesta is a trusted global partner in the healthcare industry, specializing in the manufacturing, marketing, and export of branded and private-label healthcare and cosmetic products. With cutting-edge manufacturing facilities in India, advanced R&D centers, and a strong international presence across multiple countries, we deliver innovative, reliable, and affordable healthcare solutions to meet the evolving needs of global markets.

Over the years, we have successfully built an extensive international network, exporting to over 25 countries—including the U.S., Europe, Africa, and the Middle East—and serving partners across pharmaceuticals, nutraceuticals, hygiene, sexual health, Ayurveda, and cosmeceuticals.

Today, as part of our continued growth and engagement within the global healthcare ecosystem, Medesta has also expanded into the events and knowledge-sharing space. We now proudly operate as a Professional Conference Organizer (PCO) for healthcare-focused events, with a special emphasis on emerging markets like the Middle East and Africa. Leveraging our healthcare expertise and global relationships, we curate impactful, insight-driven conferences that bring together thought leaders, researchers, and policy-makers to shape the future of health.

Headquartered in the UAE, with offices across key global regions, Medesta is uniquely positioned to bridge the worlds of healthcare innovation and professional networking, creating platforms for collaboration and global exchange.

MEDEVENTS - Healthcare Conferences & Summits
“Converging Healthcare Minds”

MedEvents is the global conferences and events division of Medesta, dedicated to organizing world-class healthcare summits, medical congresses, and wellness forums across key emerging markets. With a strong focus on the Middle East and Africa, MedEvents brings together healthcare professionals, policymakers, industry leaders, and researchers to foster collaboration, innovation, and actionable insights.

From scientific symposiums and nutraceutical roundtables to traditional medicine forums and public health dialogues, MedEvents curates knowledge platforms that shape the future of global healthcare. Backed by Medesta’s international presence and domain expertise, we offer end-to-end conference management as a Professional Conference Organizer (PCO)—ensuring excellence, engagement, and global reach.

Medesto formulates and delivers premium & science-backed nutraceutical products designed to enhance health, vitality & overall well-being across all life stages. MEDESOLACE - Ayurveda

MEDEATOMS - Cosmeceutical
“Cosmeceutical Elegance…Elevating Beauty with Science”

Medeatoms crafts advanced skincare, haircare, & dermatological solutions infused with cutting- edge bioactives & postbiotics. Designed to nourish, protect, & enhance natural beauty, our formulations reflect a steadfast commitment to innovation, efficacy & excellence in beauty & personal care.

HYGEA - Healthcare Disposable
“For Every Stage of life… Comfort, Care & Confidence”

At Hygea, a division of Medesta Healthcare with a special focus on hygiene and sexual health, we seamlessly blend innovation, quality & care to set new benchmarks in disposable healthcare solutions. ZEST PHARMA - Finished Formulations
“Accessibility and Affordability for all”

Our pharmaceutical division specializes in manufacturing & exporting high-quality finished formulations across various dosage forms, including dry powders, liquid injections, oral tablets, capsules, soft gels, syrups, drops, sachets, creams, ointments & specialized treatments for oncology. We operate with all necessary accreditations, registrations & dossiers, ensuring compliance with global regulatory standards. Our manufacturing units have various international accreditations & CTD dossier availability for more than 600 products.

WYLO - Surgical & Hospital Equipment's

MEET THE FOUNDER
Dr. Monica Sood Bhatia

A Visionary Leader Shaping Global Healthcare

Dr. Monica Sood Bhatia , a medical professional and Harvard Business School graduate, brings over 25 years of leadership experience in the healthcare and technology sectors across India, the UAE, and international markets. As an innovative global business leader, Dr. Monica Sood Bhatia has successfully steered organizations at the intersection of science, strategy, and service. She has served as a strategic consultant to renowned healthcare brands including Tata 1mg, Doxy.me (USA), Pristyn Care, Panacea Biotec, Cimglobal, and FCB Ulka and played a pivotal role in launching and expanding transformative healthcare initiatives. For over 15 years, she led the India and Middle East operations of CIMS Medica, Asia-Pacific’s largest multichannel healthcare media company. Her journey also includes launching and publishing numerous medical journals, newspapers, and pioneering healthcare exhibitions and conferences that continue to influence the global medical landscape
