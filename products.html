FAQ

Q1. WHAT IS THIRD-PARTY MANUFACTURING?
Third party or contract manufacturing is referred to the outsourcing of pharmaceutical products or to get products manufactured from other manufacturing units with your own brand names.
Q2. HOW MANY PRODUCTS DO WE HAVE AT OUR DOMESTIC BRANCH?
We have 650+ products in our portfolio.
Q3. WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN ETHICAL, PCD AND GENERIC MEDICINES?
Generic medicines are the same as compare to ethical medicines in all aspects i.e. strength, dosage, efficacy, results, uses, etc. just that they are cheaper in price as compared to branded or ethical products. Same is the case with PCD franchise medicines; the same medicine is used as in case of generic and ethical. The only difference is the channel of distribution. We cannot say which medicine is better, the medicine is the same, the manufacturing process is the same and the origin of raw material is the same. The difference is created by the marketing team.
